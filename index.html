Frequently Asked Questions

India's Next Voice helps aspiring singers create their identity in the music industry through expert mentorship, original song creation, stage performances, and strong digital visibility. If you're passionate about singing, this is where your journey truly begins.

How do I register?

You can register by filling out the registration form on this page. It's completely free to register and submit your audition.

Can I submit more than one video?

Yes, you can submit up to 3 videos to showcase different styles and ranges of your singing.

Is there any audition fee?

No, there is no audition fee. Registration and participation in India's Next Voice are completely free.

Do I need professional equipment to record my audition?

No, you don't need professional equipment. A clear recording from your smartphone is sufficient; focus on your voice quality and performance.

Age limit?

There is no upper age limit. Participants must be at least 13 years old.

How long should my audition video be?

Your audition video should be between 1 to 3 minutes long to keep it concise and engaging.

What format of video is allowed?

Supported formats include MP4, AVI for video and MP3, WAV for audio. File size should not exceed 100MB.

Will I receive feedback if not selected?

All participants receive constructive feedback via email, regardless of selection, to help improve their skills.

How will shortlisting happen?

Shortlisting is conducted by our expert panel of judges based on vocal technique, originality, emotional delivery, and overall performance.

Can international participants apply?

Yes, international participants are welcome to apply and participate in this global talent hunt.
